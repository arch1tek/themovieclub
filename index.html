about.

The Movie Club was established as a place to provide a community for film lovers that has evolved to include a filmmaking wing and now actively analyses films and generates discourse on them.

It now produces podcasts, video essays, reviews in different formats; makes films; particpates in film festivals; conducts film-making workshops and much more.

It has increased by leaps and bounds since it's humble beginning as a club that was intended just for screening movies and looks forward to create a good film culture in the campus and produce talented directors, writers, editors (including sfx, vfx artists, animators) and cinematographers.

slumber party.


We host fun movie screenings late into the night where you can come and chill with your friends. During fests, we have different rooms for various genres where we screen 2-3 movies each night.
